Isaac Cheat Sheet (Exodus)

Exodus Items (35)

  • Beehive

    ItemID: 530

    "Hive minded"

    • Turns tears into bees that wiggle around and home in on enemies.

    • Bees have infinite range.

      Type: Passive

      Item Pool: Item Room

  • Sad Tears

    ItemID: 531

    "Even they are crying"

    • -0.4 Shot Speed Down.

    • Tears have a luck-based chance to shoot a fast tear in a random direction.

      Type: Passive

      Item Pool: Item Room

  • Busted Pipe

    ItemID: 532

    "Leaky tears"

    • Tears have a chance to leave a trail of dark blue creep that damages enemies.

      Type: Passive

      Item Pool: Item Room

  • Unholy Mantle

    ItemID: 533

    "Satanic defence"

    • When Isaac gets hit, all enemies in the room are heavily damaged, this effect recharges on the next floor.

    • Completing a floor without taking damage grants two black hearts.

      Type: Passive

      Item Pool: Item Room

  • Tech 360

    ItemID: 534

    "Tech bubble"

    • x1.25 Range Multiplier.

    • Isaac now fires expanding tech rings instead of tears that are locked to his position.

    • When the rings reach a certain size (based on range), they burst into tech lasers.

      Type: Passive

      Item Pool: Item Room

  • Paper Cut

    ItemID: 535

    "Ouch!"

    • When Isaac uses a card, all enemies in the room are damaged and given the bleed effect.

    • Using cards instantly kills Stoneys. Paper beats rock!

      Type: Passive

      Item Pool: Item Room

  • Forget Me Later

    ItemID: 536

    "I still can't remember"

    • On a random upcoming trapdoor, Isaac will redo the floor instead of heading to the next one.

    • This only happens once.

      Type: Passive

      Item Pool: Item Room

  • Dragon Breath

    ItemID: 537

    "Charged fireballs"

    • Isaac charges up a fireball that burns the first enemy it touches, and then explodes after traveling further.

      Type: Passive

      Item Pool: Item Room

  • Cobalt Necklace

    ItemID: 538

    "Win streak"

    • Completing rooms without taking damage builds up a counter.

    • With each perfected room, Isaac is given a damage up.

    • Taking damage resets the counter. The number can reach up to 101, after that, it just shows an infinity symbol, but still increases damage.

    • This item is a reference to Twitch streamer, CobaltStreak. We're big fans!

      Type: Passive

      Item Pool: Item Room

  • Pig Blood

    ItemID: 539

    "Tastes like apples"

    • Buying a passive item from a devil deal restocks it with another devil item.

      Type: Passive

      Item Pool: Item Room

  • Dad's Boots

    ItemID: 540

    "Squish"

    • +0.1 Speed Up.

    • Allows Isaac to step on small enemies such as spiders.

      Type: Passive

      Item Pool: Item Room

  • Mysterious Mustache

    ItemID: 541

    "Where did it come from?"

    • Buying something from the shop has a 50% chance to heal Isaac for half of a red heart.

    • There is a 1% chance for Isaac to get a full refund when purchasing an item.

    • This item is a reference to Mustache from Enter the Gungeon.

      Type: Passive

      Item Pool: Item Room

  • Cursed Metronome

    ItemID: 542

    "Tock tick"

    • Getting hit activates the Metronome effect.

      Type: Passive

      Item Pool: Item Room

  • Big Scissors

    ItemID: 543

    "Running with them!"

    • +0.2 Speed Up.

    • When Isaac taks damage, he loses the 0.2 speed upgrade for the room.

    • Getting hit also gives a blood creep trail and +2 tears up for the room.

      Type: Passive

      Item Pool: Item Room

  • Welcome Mat

    ItemID: 544

    "No place like home"

    • Upon entering a room, a welcome mat is placed on the doorway.

    • Standing near this welcome mat gives a +3 tears up.

      Type: Passive

      Item Pool: Item Room

  • Gluttony's Stomach

    ItemID: 545

    "Rewards overeating"

    • Picking up red hearts that you don't need fills you up.

    • If you pick up enough, you get an empty red heart container.

    • This item is heavily inspired off of the part system from Monolith.

      Type: Passive

      Item Pool: Item Room

  • Possessed Bombs

    ItemID: 546

    "Controlled bombs"

    • +1 Bomb.

    • Isaac can control his bombs after placing them with the shooting controls.

      Type: Passive

      Item Pool: Item Room

  • Buttrot

    ItemID: 547

    "Blight shot"

    • Tears have a luck-based chance to be Buttrot tears that knockback-fart on enemies and inflict the Blight effect.

    • Blighted enemies are slowed but cannot be knocked back.

      Type: Passive

      Item Pool: Item Room

  • Sling

    ItemID: 548

    "Size matters"

    • Enemies with a sling icon above them take more damage.

    • The bigger the enemy, the more damage Isaac does.

      Type: Passive

      Item Pool: Item Room

  • Yin

    ItemID: 549

    "Negativity"

      Type: Passive

      Item Pool: Item Room
